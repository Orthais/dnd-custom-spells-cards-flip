Sort Treasure
Alteration - 2nd Level
Range: 10 yards/level Components: V, S
Duration: Special Casting Time: 1 turn
Area of Effect: 102 yards/level Saving Throw: None
This spell takes unsorted treasure and sorts it into individual neat piles based on their classification. Coins will be split into copper, silver, gold, etc. For weapons and armour, Swords, Blunt, Shields, Armour sets, individual armour pieces, etc. Jewelry, pouches, and the like are in one pile. Gems are in their own pile separated by type. Art also gets its own pile. Miscellaneous/uncategorized objects are loosely collected together.

This is test copy.

Ice Spike
Evocation - 3rd level
Range: 10 yards + 10 yards/level Components:V, S, M
Duration: Instantaneous Casting Time: 3
Area of Effect: Single Target Saving Throw: 1/2
A large 6ft long ice spike forms above the caster's head and is hurled at a single opponent. Target takes 1d4 per level of caster and must make a saving throw versus spell. If the target succeeds their saving throw, they take half damage. The material component for this is a thin rod of frosted glass.

This is test copy.

Frost Grenade
Evocation - 3rd level
Range: 10 yards + 10 yards/level Components: V, S, M
Duration: Instantaneous Casting Time: 3
Area of Effect: 20ft radius Saving Throw: 1/2
An explosive burst of frost and ice that shivers forth and coats everything it touches with a thick coating of frost. Those caught in the blast take 1d6 points of damage per caster level to a max of tenth and must save versus spell, on a success targets take half damage. Unlike Fireball, this spell does not expand in the same nature when cast in small areas or tight corridors. But it may freeze or slow those caught in its blast. Restricting movement, or even stopping a target from unsheathing a weapon. The material component for this is a pinch of dried peppermint and a drop of mercury.

This is test copy.
